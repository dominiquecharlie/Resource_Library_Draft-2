Teacher Time Analysis
Resource Library

A guided process to help district leaders identify priorities and connect to research-aligned strategies and TEA initiatives within the Effective District Framework.

What this is

A step-by-step process to help your district:

  • Identify a priority need within a specific lever
  • Explore aligned strategies and initiatives
  • Understand the staffing and capacity required for success

What this isn't

  • This tool is not a list of programs
  • It is not a diagnostic tool
  • It will not analyze your data for you
  • It will not create an implementation plan
